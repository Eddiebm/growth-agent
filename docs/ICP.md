# ICP — Ideal Customer Profile

## Target

- **Industry:** SEO agencies, marketing automation consultancies
- **Size:** 10–100 employees
- **Geo:** US, UK, Canada, Australia
- **Titles:** Founder, CEO, Head of Operations, Director of Client Services

## Qualifiers (must have ≥2)

- Runs client campaigns at scale (10+ active clients)
- Uses tools like HubSpot, Semrush, Ahrefs, or Zapier
- Has a services revenue model (not pure SaaS)

## Disqualifiers

- Pure enterprise (>500 employees) — long sales cycle
- Solo freelancer — can't afford retainer
- Competitor building their own agent platform
- Generic info@ emails only, no named contact found
- Domain age < 6 months

## Pain points we solve

1. Manual lead gen eating founder time
2. Inconsistent outreach quality across reps
3. Can't scale personalized outreach without hiring

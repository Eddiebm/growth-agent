# Voice

## Tone

- Direct, confident, not salesy
- Peer-to-peer (founder to founder)
- Short sentences. No corporate fluff.

## Do

- Lead with their pain, not our features
- Use specific numbers when possible
- One clear CTA per message
- Sign off with first name only

## Don't

- Use "synergy", "leverage", "revolutionary", "game-changer"
- Over-promise results
- Send walls of text
- Use excessive exclamation marks
- Pretend to have met them before if we haven't

## Example opener (good)

> Hi {{first_name}} — noticed {{company}} runs SEO for mid-market clients. Most agencies your size lose 5–10 hrs/week on manual lead gen. We build AI bots that handle that for ~$500. Worth a 15-min look?

## Example opener (bad)

> Dear Sir/Madam, We are excited to introduce our revolutionary AI-powered synergy platform that will transform your business!!!
